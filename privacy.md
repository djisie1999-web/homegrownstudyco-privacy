Homegrown Study Co — Privacy Policy

  Last updated: 25 May 2026

  This application ("Homegrown Study Co Analytics") is an internal tool operated by Homegrown Study Co to read analytics data from our own Pinterest business account via the Pinterest API.

  Data we access:
  - Pin impressions, saves, pin clicks, and outbound clicks on pins published by Homegrown Study Co
  - Board metadata for boards owned by Homegrown Study Co
  - Account-level metadata for the Homegrown Study Co Pinterest business account

  Data we do NOT access:
  - Any data from Pinterest users other than the Homegrown Study Co account owner
  - Personal identifying information of other Pinterest users
  - Private messages or any non-analytics data

  Storage and sharing:
  - Analytics data is stored locally in a private database for internal performance analysis
  - No data is shared with third parties
  - No data is sold or used for advertising

  Retention:
  - Data is retained indefinitely for historical trend analysis. The data subject (Homegrown Study Co) may delete the local database at any time.
